Teacher’s Day

Event Description

Teacher’s Day is a special celebration to honor the hard work, dedication, and love of teachers who guide students every day.

Reflection Questions

  1. What is the most important thing I learned from the event?
    I learned how important teachers are in shaping who we become.

  2. How can I apply what I learned in real-life situations?
    I can show respect and gratitude to teachers not only during the event but every day.

  3. Did I actively participate in the event? How?
    Yes, I joined our class performance and helped make thank-you cards for our teachers.

  4. If I were to teach this topic to a classmate, how would I explain it?
    I’d say Teacher’s Day is about showing appreciation to the people who help us learn and grow.

  5. Why is it important to have an event per subject?
    It teaches us values like respect, gratitude, and teamwork in fun ways outside the classroom.
