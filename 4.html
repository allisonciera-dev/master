Araling Panlipunan Month

Event Description

Araling Panlipunan Month celebrates Philippine history, culture, and citizenship. It reminds students to value our past and understand our role in shaping the future.

Reflection Questions

  1. What is the most important thing I learned from the event?
    I learned that knowing our history helps us understand who we are and where we came from.

  2. How can I apply what I learned in real-life situations?
    I can be more patriotic, respectful of our culture, and aware of our country’s issues.

  3. Did I actively participate in the event? How?
    Yes, I helped my class with a presentation about national heroes.

  4. If I were to teach this topic to a classmate, how would I explain it?
    I’d say it’s about learning from the past to build a better future for our country.

  5. Why is it important to have an event per subject?
    It lets us apply what we study in creative ways and helps us remember it better.
